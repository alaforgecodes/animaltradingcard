Barn Owl

The barn owl is usually nocturnal, but in some areas, such as Great Britain, it also hunts during the day.

  • Scientific Name: Tyto alba
  • Average Length: 13 - 15 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 4 years
  • Habitat: Every continent except Antartica

Barn owls, with their distinctive white faces and brown backs, are the most widely distributed species of owl in the world. They have an average maximum length of 15 inches, and their scientific name translates to "white night owl."
